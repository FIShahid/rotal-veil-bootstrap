Our Services

Full Service Planning

The day you say "I do" is one of the most special moments in your life. We specialize in full service planning where we handle all the details from start to finish from location scouting, putting together a stellar team of creative partners, styling the look of your event to managing every last detail giving you the ultimate peace of mind. With our years of designing experience, you not only get a planner but also a design team to style every detail of your event.

Floral & Event Design

Established and recognized as one of the top design firms in the country, our clients are all about the fine details. We offer full event decor services for all kinds of celebrations. We take pride in taking a simple idea and creating something magical with it. Our professional and creative design team and vendors are onboard on one mission, to specifically deliver extraordinary results for your grand event.

Destination Planning

For discerning clients seeking a romantic destination wedding or celebration, we are ready to jet set to your dream destination offering full service destination planning. This experience offers a hands on approach orchestrating an unforgettable experience. We will help you create an unforgettable destination event for you and your guests.
